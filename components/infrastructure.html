01

Community Broadband

Dedicated internet via MMBI fiber at the Garberville point of interconnect, carried by licensed microwave to 4 ridge nodes at elevation, distributed by point-to-multipoint RF to 8 valley nodes across the watershed. 100 Mbps symmetric. No throttling. No data caps. No commercial carrier terms.

02

Private LTE Cellular

A CBRS Band 48 private cellular network built from scratch for this territory — not a repeater, not a booster, not a workaround. Real LTE bars on community members' existing phones. Fieldwork-issued SIM cards for fire departments, anchor institutions, and residents in areas commercial carriers will never reach.

03

Emergency Communications

A resilient LoRaWAN sensor and signaling mesh that operates fully independently of the primary network. Environmental monitoring, fire detection telemetry, and an emergency signaling floor that keeps working when primary systems are degraded.

04

Off-Grid Solar Power

Every node runs entirely on solar with LiFePO4 battery backup providing days of autonomy. Dual-fuel inverter generators with fire-rated enclosures provide autonomous backup at every ridge and valley node. No PG&E dependency anywhere in the network. No grid failure risk when it matters most.
